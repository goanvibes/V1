About FAC

Rooted in Pilar. Open to the world.

Fr. Agnel College was established in June 1991 by the Xaverian Educational Society, Pilar. The college stands in the historic village of Pilar, once known as Govapuri or Gopakapattana during the Kadamba reign, and continues to serve students from across Goa with a focus on competence, character and community.
